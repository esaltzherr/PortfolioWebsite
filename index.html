Game Projects

Where's my gun?

This is a wave-surviving game where after each wave you get to upgrade an ability you have, but the ability that you don't choose to upgrade will be disabled for the wave, so you need to be careful with balancing what you truly want.

As the player mechanics developer I implemented the movement, combat, and abilities.

Made in JavaScript's Phaser Framework, as a 3 person team in 2 weeks.

Dark Throne

This is a 2D platforming metroidvania where a knight is trapped in a dungeon and must fight and climb their way out by finding new abilities, with a main focus on the combat and fun movement.

As the lead programmer and project lead, I worked on the player movement, abilities, and the back-end of the game including the saving, scene management, managing and helping my group members.

Made in Unity and uploaded to Steam.

Pencil Pusher

This is an endless runner where you grab letters from the page and make words in order to survive the eraser that is coming at you.

I worked on the word checking implementation and the enemy that is chasing you.

Made in Phaser as a small 3 person team in 2 weeks.
